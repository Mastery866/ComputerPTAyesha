About Our School

Philosophy

Catholic education is excellence-driven, learner-centered, transformative, holistic, and oriented towards service in the example of Jesus Christ.

Vision

Saint Rose of Lima Catholic School, Inc. is an educational community inspired by the spirituality of Saint Rose of Lima, centred on the person, life, and mission of Jesus Christ, committed to a transformative and holistic Catholic education towards the fullness of life.

Mission

1. Boldly promote the living out of Catholic faith and identity. 2. Sustainably nurture a school community in the spirit of servant leadership. 3. Vigorously focus on holistic learner development. 4. Innovatively provide opportunities for value-added services and programs that foster a faith-filled learning environment. 5. Decisively uphold Christian stewardship of resources for institutional advancement and viability.

Core Values

  • Faith in God
  • Integrity
  • Respect
  • Excellence
  • Service
